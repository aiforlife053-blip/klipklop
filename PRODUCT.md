# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Indonesian creators, editors, and solo operators who want to turn YouTube videos or podcasts into short-form clips from a local desktop-style web app. They use it while preparing content batches and need the workflow to stay clear during long AI/video processing jobs.

## Product Purpose

KlipKlop automates clip discovery, subtitle generation, portrait/landscape formatting, and export management for short-form video. Success means users can paste a YouTube URL, choose a few practical settings, monitor progress, preview results, and download usable clips with minimal manual editing.

## Brand Personality

Minimalist dark, calm, practical. The interface should feel like a focused creator tool: low glare, direct controls, readable status, and no decorative noise.

## Anti-references

- Busy SaaS dashboards with excessive cards, shadows, gradients, and gamified metrics.
- Overly bright creator tools that rely on loud orange everywhere.
- Decorative glassmorphism, heavy borders, or fake depth that distracts from the clipping workflow.
- AI-template layouts that look generic instead of purpose-built for video processing.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the creation flow obvious: URL, settings, process, preview, download.
2. Prefer calm dark surfaces with strong contrast and restrained accent usage.
3. Show processing state clearly without competing with the form or results.
4. Make result cards scannable and preview-first.
5. Remove controls that do not help the current task.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA. Maintain strong text/background contrast, visible focus states, keyboard-accessible controls, readable placeholder text, and reduced-motion-safe interactions.

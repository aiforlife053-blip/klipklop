# KlipKlop

## Register
Product

## Purpose
Local web tool for creators to generate one vertical short clip from a YouTube URL, preview it, then save, upload, or delete it.

## Users
Content creators editing quickly on a desktop in a focused, low-light workflow.

## Personality
Fast, compact, direct.

## Principles
- Keep the generation form minimal.
- Default hidden technical choices to the safest local workflow.
- Make video preview and actions the center of the task.
- Preserve accessibility and keyboard focus for critical actions.

## Defaults
- Highlight finder: Gemini API.
- Subtitle engine: Groq Whisper API.
- Speech-to-text model: whisper-large-v3-turbo.
- Subtitle language: Indonesian.
- Screen size: 9:16.
- Captions: always on.
- Output: one clip per run.
